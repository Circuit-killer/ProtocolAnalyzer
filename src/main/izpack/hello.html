Welcome to ProtocolAnalyzer ${version}


ProtocolAnalyzer is a simple tool for viewing and analyzing digital signals via the microphone input. It is intended as an aid when writing decoders for slow pulse based protocols such as IR or RF remotes. ProtocolAnalyzer is written by: Stefan Strömberg

ProtocolAnalyzer is open source and is released under Gnu General public licence version 3
You are welcome to visit http://www.nethome.nu for more information about the tool and other connecting projects.
